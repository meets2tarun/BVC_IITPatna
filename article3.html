Blissful life with Krishna Consciousness

The people who want to live peacefully and happily, they must practice Krishna consciousness. Now, the question arises, What is Krishna consciousness? It is the absence of Maya. To live in Krishna consciousness, we have to be attached with Krishna, not with materialistic things. We have to always love Krishna. We should know about ourself, Who am I?. First, we have to realize that I am a soul. If we have self realization then definitely we can realize the God. And also if we realize the God , then we can live peacefully for the whole life.

Each soul has an eternal, dynamic, blissful relationship with the Supreme soul, the Supreme Controller of universe, the Supreme Personality of Godhead Lord Krishna. Our actual life is not in this material world but in the spiritual world with Krishna.

Krishna Consciousness is not a dull, dry, ritualistic religion. Krishna Consciousness means chanting the holy names, always remembering Krishna in every situation, dancing in ecstasy, feasting on Krishna Prasadam, associating with saintly devotees, serving the Supreme Personality of Godhead in his Deity form, appreciating the unsurpassable beauty of the Deity, hearing about the Potencies and Pastimes of Krishna and preaching His Glories.

If a person practices Krishna Consciousness, then one becomes totally free from mundane desires like lust, greed, and envy. Thus, we will get blissful life in this material world. Therefore every thoughtful person should immediately take up Krishna Consciousness. with full heart and soul.
