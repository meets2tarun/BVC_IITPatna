RAJGIR

Rajgir is situated 84 Km. away from Patna. We had one day trip to Rajgir on 22nd March 2015. The journey was short and pleasant. Delicious Prasadam was made by Dhiren Nayak, Lucky Kumar and other club members. The coordinators for this trip were Deepak Chandapure and Raviteja B. Vishwa Shanti Stupa, situated 1000 ft. above the ground on a hill, is the main attraction. All the members decided to climb the hill by foot and it was really exciting and adventurous. It is a magnificent place with scenic beauty and spiritual aura. The whole journey was made memorable by the continuous kirtana and the talks given by Dr. Ranjan Kumar Behera. After visiting some nearby places, we returned to Patna.

SRI MAYAPUR DHAM

Mayapur, an island in the Navadweep district of West Bengal, is the birth place of Lord Caitanya Mahaprabhu. It is due to His causeless mercy that we had the chance to visit Mayapur. We boarded the train from Patna to Vardhman. Mayapur Devotees arranged temple taxi and we safely reached Sri Mayapur Dham. After reaching Mayapur, we visited ISKCON Shri Chandrodaya Mandir where Panchatatva, Narsmha Dev and Radha Madhava with Ashta Sakhis reside. Just a glimpse of them filled our hearts with ecstasy. After the darsana, we attended a video lecture of A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, and then visited many popular & sacred places like Bhakti Vinod Thakur's school and Shri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu's birthplace. They are the most important places among the Vaishnavas, and so are always crowded with devotees. The Mayapur Dhama is the original home of all the living entities, Vaikuntha on earth. We returned with blissful hearts, filled with unparalleled love for Lord Caitanya Mahaprabhu.

SHRI Krishna JANMASHTAMI

Janmashtami, the appearance day of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Sri Krishna is the most important festival for one and all. It was on this eighth day (ashtami) of the Krsna paksha of Shravana month that Sri Krsna appeared in Mathura. Lord Sri Krsna was born to Mother Devaki at midnight in the prison house of Kansa, and had shown His four handed form to His parents just after His birth. The Bhaktivedanta club celebrated Janmashtami with great enthusiasm. The preparations had started a month earlier, with various committees setup for different purposes. On the occasion of Janmashtami the Club organized Vedic quiz and painting competitions. The Janmashtami day was celebrated with many cultural activities such as devotional dance, kirtan, drama and cultural fancy dress. The melodious sankirtana and the enlightening seminar by Dr. Ranjan Kumar Behera on Krisna Consciousness were the major attractions of the night. There was active participation from more than 800 people from all over Patna, along with the IIT Patna fraternity. Prizes and certificates were distributed among the winners and participants of different events . The program ended with mid night kirtan and Abhishekam of the Lord, followed by delicious prasadam prepared by Dr. Manoranjan Kar. The atmosphere was charged with immense love for Krsna, which seamlessly mingled into everyones hearts. Dr. Sanjoy K. Parida coordinated the whole program.

VISITS OF VAISNAVAS

H. H. PRABHODANANDA SWAMI MAHARAJA

H. H. Prabhodananda Saraswati Maharaja is a Tridandi Sanyasi under the flagship of ISKCON. He is a versatile and dynamic preacher of Indian Culture based on Krishna Consciousness through out the world. His preaching area includes India (Patna, Delhi, Mathura, Rourkela etc.) and abroad (United Kingdom USA, SA, Durban etc.).He is a permanent resident of Vrajabhumi. Sometimes he visits our club out of his causeless mercy to distribute the love of Godhead and share the loving Pastimes of the Supreme Lord Sri Krishna. He visited in September 2014 and delivered many lectures on Vaishnava etiquettes and discussed about stum-bling blocks on the path of devotional service. He gave scholarly references from many scriptures and discussed how these stumbling blocks should be well known so that we can remove them from root. At the end he performed very nice dancing kirtan in his melodious voice. Finally, after spending four memorable days with us, he left for his next preaching destination. The club is always indebted to Maharaja for his visit to Patna.

H G SUNDAR GOPAL DAS

HG Sundar Gopal Das visited IIT Patna on 27th October 2014. He delivered a seminar on How Spirituality and Technology is interconnected. He explained the topic very nicely with the help of videos. The session ended with delicious Prasadam. Prabhupada, the founder acharya of ISKCON was always very eager that scientists and technologists understand and appreciate the value of spirituality in benefitting humanity. He therefore wrote books like Easy journey to other planets and established the BI or BhaktiVedanta Institute .

H.H. Bhaktiashraya Vaishnava Swami MAHARAJ

H. H. Bhaktiashraya Vaishnava Swami Maharaj is a Tridandi sanyasi. He regularly visits our club and is a resident of Vraja Dhama. After long time he blessed us with his august presence in the beginning of this year. He is well known for presenting antique stories about different holy places of the India. In other words, he is a living encyclopedia of all holy places of India. This time he presented stories on glories of Lord Jagannatha and also gave lectures on many topics like Money is sweeter than Honey, Gaura Avtar, Sharnagati, Strongest among senses, Values of human life, Who is God etc. He delivered a institute lecture at IIT Patna and systematically he discussed the values of human life from the scripture.
